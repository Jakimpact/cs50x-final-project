One of the special features of crypto assets is the wide variety of way you can buy them, but also hold them. Whether you use an exchange, a web-based or a physical wallet, it is sometimes difficult to keep track of all these assets spread over these services allowing for their conservation.

The objective of this site is to provide you a tool to create a database of the different crypto assets you own, allowing a global vision on them. Once created, you will be able to visualize your assets in various tables offering statistics on them but also your overall portfolio.

For security sake, no personal data is requested. So be sure to download your database and store it on your PC, otherwise you won't find it back when connecting to the site later.
Finally, you can import this database to update it or check the tables.
